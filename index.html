Frequently Asked Questions
Who is an Authorised Partner?
An Authorised Person is a person who is registered with SEBI as such and is affiliated to a member of a recognised stock exchange.
What does an Authorised Partner do?
The main role of an Authorised Person is to onboard clients under him & help them manage their investments. Based on the service provided, brokerage will be charged to the client and will be shared with the Authorised Partner.
How much do I need to invest to become an Authorised Partner?
We charge ZERO security deposit, when most of the other stock brokers ask for a hefty security deposit. You’ll need to pay only the registration charges, as applied by NSE and MCX.
How much time it takes to become an Authorised Partner with Astha Trade?
Becoming an Authorised Partner with us is completely paperless. You can submit your documents in 10 mins which is submitted with exchange. Exchange may take 2-3 working days to process the application. This is the fastest registration service available in the industry.
What is the eligibility to become an authorized person?
  1. Should be a citizen of India
  2. Should not be less than 18 years of age
  3. Should not have been convicted of any offence involving fraud and dishonesty
  4. Should have good reputation and character
  5. Should have passed at least 10th standard or equivalent examination
What are the documents required for registration?
  1. Address proof: Aadhaar
  2. Educational qualification certificate: Any passing certificate for 10th and above
  3. PAN
